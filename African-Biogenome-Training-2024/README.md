## üìò Overview

The **African BioGenome Project North Africa Workshop** introduced participants to **biodiversity genomics, genomic technologies, bioinformatics approaches, and applications in bioeconomy**. Participants were exposed to **theoretical lectures, practical workshops, and expert talks**, providing opportunities to:

* Understand **biodiversity genomics** and its relevance to bioeconomy policies in North Africa
* Learn about **genomic technologies**, sequencing methods, and bioinformatics pipelines
* Explore **marine, agricultural, and livestock genomics** applications
* Participate in **practical sessions** on sequencing, data analysis, and computational tools
* Engage with **keynote speakers and experts** from academia, industry, and international organizations

> Detailed session notes are available in the [Event-Notes folder](./Event-Notes).
